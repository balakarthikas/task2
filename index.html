EXNORA QUALITY CIRCLE

ExNoRa Quality Circles are for any office or factory set up, where employees voluntarily assemble, identify common environmental problems in their office premises, campus, complex etc., find solutions by brain storming and either they themselves bring the improvement, or recommended the same to the higher authorities for suitable action.

ExNoRa Quality Circle can introduce a system of separation of trash as compostable, recyclable and others. Composting can be done with in the office. Paper can be shredded with the use of a shredder. The circle will also be responsible for introduction of rain harvesting in the office campus.
